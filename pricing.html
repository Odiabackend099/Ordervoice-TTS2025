Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 7-day free trial work?

Simply sign up for any plan and you'll get full access for 7 days completely free. No credit card required to start. If you're satisfied, we'll automatically convert to a paid plan. Otherwise, cancel anytime with zero charges.

What happens if I exceed my monthly call limit?

We'll notify you when you approach 80% of your limit. You can either upgrade to a higher plan or purchase additional call credits at ₦350 per call. We never cut off service mid-month.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer custom enterprise plans?

Absolutely. For businesses needing more than 1,000 calls/month, multiple locations, or specialized features, contact our sales team at support@ordervoice.ai or WhatsApp +234 814 199 5397.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Paystack, bank transfers, and for Business plans, we offer monthly invoicing. All prices are in Nigerian Naira (₦).

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees. No hidden costs. You pay only the monthly subscription. We handle all setup, integration, and training at no extra charge.
